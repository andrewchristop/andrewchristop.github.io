New To AAUW?

AAUW is a growing non-profit organization that has been advocating for women and girls since 1881. With our numerous branches, officials, members, etc., we aim to advocate educational, social, economic, and political positions and opportunities for women across the United States and its territories.


How to join?

In order to become a member, you must hold an associate’s (or equivalent), bachelor’s, or higher degree from a regionally accredited college or university. If that is the case, follow our page to the membership application and enter your information. In no time, you will be accepted as one of many members in AAUW!
